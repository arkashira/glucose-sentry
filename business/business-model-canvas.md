# Business Model Canvas — glucose-sentry

> FX: **1 USD ≈ 36 THB**. Hardware + AI-SaaS hybrid. Key strategic call baked in: non-invasive optical/RF glucose is a category littered with failed accuracy claims — so this canvas positions the device as **adjunctive/trend-grade first**, with the clinical-dosing market gated behind real MARD validation.

## Customer Segments
- **Pre-diabetic / metabolic-anxious consumers (beachhead)** — TH ~9M pre-diabetic, ~5.2M diagnosed; highest convenience WTP, lowest regulatory bar.
- **Type 2 non-insulin diabetics** — under-monitored, hate fingersticks, CGM too costly.
- **Insurers / corporate wellness (B2B2C)** — AIA, Bupa, SSO, self-insured multinationals.
- **Clinics & diabetes educators** — channel + validation data source.
- **NOT yet: Type 1 insulin-dependent** — dosing needs <10% MARD + clearance.

## Value Propositions
- "Know your sugar without bleeding" — zero consumables vs. CGM's **$60–120 / 2,160–4,320 THB per sensor every 10–14 days**.
- Continuous AI trend alerts + lifestyle nudges.
- Honest published-MARD accuracy tiering (trust as moat).
- Open API + clinic EHR dashboard.
- One-time cost, no pain — kills the #1 adherence killer.

## Revenue Streams (headline numbers)
- Hardware: **$199–349 / 7,200–12,600 THB** (BOM <$60, ~65–75% margin)
- Consumer SaaS: **$9.99/mo / 360 THB**
- B2B seats: **$15–30/employee/yr / 540–1,080 THB**
- Clinic license: **$99–299/mo / 3,600–10,800 THB**

## Cost Structure (the make-or-break line)
- Clinical validation: **$300K–1.5M / 10.8M–54M THB** — existential.
- Regulatory + ISO 13485: **$150K–600K / 5.4M–21.6M THB**.
- Hardware COGS <$60 + tooling.

**One strategic flag for the chain:** the single biggest risk to this product isn't market demand — it's **accuracy/regulatory feasibility**. Every block above assumes we lead with a wellness/trend claim and earn the clinical claim through validation spend. If the architect/validation step can't credibly hit adjunctive-grade MARD on non-invasive sensing, this becomes a NO-GO regardless of how attractive the canvas reads.
